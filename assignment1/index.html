About Me


Todd Croak-Falen is a novelist, screenwriter, and (developing) web developer. He has published one coming-of-age novel and one short Halloween story. His interests include reading, hiking, electronic music, cycling, and the occasional chess game. He has four years of Krav Maga training.


Todd lives in Mar Vista with his wife. They are expecting their first child (a girl) in early December 2016.
